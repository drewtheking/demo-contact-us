Talk to an Attorney Who Handles Your Case Personally

If you've been injured because of someone else's negligence, getting clear legal guidance shouldn't be complicated — and it shouldn't cost you anything. At the Law Offices of Michael J. Brennan, we offer free, no-obligation case evaluations to help you understand your rights, your options, and what steps to take next.

You will not be passed off to a case manager or call center. When you contact this office, you'll hear directly from Attorney Michael J. Brennan — the person who will handle your case from start to finish.

Whether you're dealing with a serious car crash, nursing home neglect, a medical error, or the wrongful death of a loved one, we're here to help you get answers.

How to Get Started

Fill out the short contact form on this page, or call the office directly. We respond quickly to inquiries and respect your time and privacy. Once we review your situation, we'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your potential case in more detail.
